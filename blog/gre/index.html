By Johnny Lee 18 January 2024

Although there are fewer and fewer universities taking GRE into account, this special journey still deserves to be shared.

The road to preparing for the GRE is really hard. For everyone who has gone through the GRE, it is truly admirable, especially for office workers. Yes, I was working at Garmin and preparing for the GRE at the same time.

There is work pressure that people in the society call work pressure, and we have to bear the academic pressure that students call it. Those who can persevere are really among the braves, the Brave.

I am very grateful to have my family, wife, and friends who support me. Whenever I am about to collapse or give up, you always support me.

Now I am pursuing a master’s degree in Computer Science at University of Southern California. I hope to have a bright future in the States.
